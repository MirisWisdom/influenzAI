How can COVID education be improved?

Answer

The Australian Government could consider investing in ongoing professional development for healthcare practitioners to ensure they have the most up-to-date knowledge and expertise on long COVID. This could involve providing regular training workshops, webinars, or online courses that cover the latest research findings and best practices for diagnosis, treatment, and management of long COVID. Additionally, the government could provide resources and support for healthcare practitioners to develop standardized referral pathways and guidelines for managing long COVID cases in primary care settings. By investing in ongoing professional development and providing practical resources and support, the Australian Government can help ensure that healthcare practitioners have the knowledge and expertise needed to provide high-quality care for patients with long COVID.
